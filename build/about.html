Background

Culturally diverse.
Open and flexible.

Born and raised in Timișoara — the highly multicultural city that sparked the Romanian revolution back in 1989 — my direct-line ancestry includes Hungarian, Serbian, Macedonian, and Romanian.

Being immersed in different cultures from a young age has nurtured my openness and cognitive flexibility. As a result, I am willing to consider alternatives to the way things are done, and I am able to see objects, people, situations, and ideas from multiple perspectives.

Vision

Solve problems.
Delight people.

Since 2002, I’ve been working with a variety of companies and non-profits. Some were large, well-established businesses, others were just starting up. Their callenges were very different, but they all stayed true to one, clear vision — solving real problems, all the while facilitating delightful experiences for people.

This vision has continued to resonate with me ever since I started my journey. After all, this is the ultimate goal of Design.

Role

Design + engineering.
A new unity.

As a designer with engineering background, my role is to help my partners understand that products should not only look good, but work even better.

For this purpose, we work closely on research and discovery, strategic planning, visual identity, interface and experience design. We also support all engineering and marketing efforts to make sure we’re delivering a cohesive experience.

During this journey, we don’t stop questioning, rethinking and reshaping the way we present ourselves to people.

Process

Understand it.
Make it work.
Make it great.

It starts with the need to improve people’s lives. A real problem is identified. Ideas are debated and distilled into a strategy. Freshly roasted coffee is brewed.

Solutions are designed, prototyped, coded, tested, and evaluated. A minimum viable product is launched. Champagne is popped.

Feedback is not ignored. More coffee is brewed. Fixes are deployed. New features are integrated. The core product is evolved and refined. High fives are exchanged.
