Andrei Györfi

Multidisciplinary designer with a passion for technology and human-computer interaction. MSc in advanced computing systems. Perfectionist. Supermodernist.

Available for hire as a no-nonsense design consultant.

Specialities: Comprehensive experience in every aspect of small- and large-scale digital product design for mobile and web, including, but not limited to, requirements engineering, discovery and ideation, UI/UX design, prototyping, developing pattern libraries and design systems.

Currently in: Timişoara, România. Say hello.

For the past 14 years, I have been working directly with a variety of companies and nonprofits. Some were large, well-established businesses, others were just starting up. Their challenges were very different, but they all stayed true to one clear vision: solving real problems, all the while facilitating delightful experiences for people. This kind of vision has continued to resonate with me ever since. After all, this is the ultimate goal of Design.
